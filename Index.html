About Me

I grew up in a small city called Lidköping, and one of my favorite childhood memories is riding my bike down to the lakeside during summer evenings. I would sit by the water, watching the sunset reflect across Lake Vänern, completely unaware that those quiet moments would later inspire my love for creativity and visual design.

Academic Interests & Hobbies

My curiosity for web development began when I took an introductory course in college. What started as a simple assignment quickly grew into a passion for solving problems, building interactive experiences, and understanding how technology shapes the world around us.

  • Video Games – I enjoy exploring different genres, especially story-driven titles. Games have taught me a lot about design, creativity, and how meaningful user experiences are built.
  • Hiking – Getting out into nature is one of my favorite ways to recharge. Long walks on forest trails or along the coastline help me clear my mind and find inspiration.
  • Photography – I love capturing small moments and beautiful landscapes. Photography encourages me to slow down, observe details, and appreciate the world from different perspectives.

Future Goals

One of my long-term goals is to become a graphic designer so I can create visually engaging and user-friendly websites that make a meaningful impact. I’m passionate about combining aesthetics with functionality, and I hope to design digital experiences that are both beautiful and intuitive for people to use.
